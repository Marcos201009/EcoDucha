About Us

We are a team of five 15-year-old students from Secundaria at High Point International School working on an integrative project focused on water conservation and awareness.

Our project analyzes water usage habits by first recording baseline consumption and students’ perceived water use through short surveys. We then implement a chronometer system that automatically stops water flow after ten minutes, encouraging users to become more aware of their water consumption. Water-use duration is monitored over a set period to observe behavioral changes caused by this time restriction. Finally, post-intervention surveys are conducted to compare actual water use with initial perceptions and evaluate improvements in awareness and conservation behavior. Through this project, we aim to promote responsible water use while developing skills in research, collaboration, and problem-solving..
